A Brief History

Liberty Christian Centre was officially launched in 1999. The Senior Pastor; Bishop Dr. Kepha Omae took a bold step of faith and obedience to the Lord and began the work with a congregation of about 25 members.

The beginning was not an easy one as the location at that time was considered a wilderness and no church was thriving at all along Mombasa road.

Despite all the prevalent challenges in the area, Bishop Omae and a few brethren who believed in his vision steered on and today we look back and say the Indeed the Lord is Ebenezer.

The Vision of LCC is to reach the unreached with the gospel of Jesus Christ and to empower them to lead fulfilled lives.

The Mission of Liberty Christian Centre is to disciple men and women in the knowledge of God to effectively transform society spiritually, socially and economically.
