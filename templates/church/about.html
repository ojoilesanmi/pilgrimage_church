A Brief History

Abundant Life Prayer and Evangelistic Ministry also known as Pilgrim Miracle Church 1st Sunday Service was held at Miracle House, Alarere Off Old Ife road, Ibadan o the 25th of December 1989 after a week long crusade. In 1990, the Lord promised to build the church a bigger place and directed the set man, Rev Ademolaami Afolayan to move the ministry location to Ososami, Ibadan while services continue at Alarere Branch.

By God's grace, the foundation for the Miracle Tower Auditorium was laid in the year 1991. and was finally dedicated in the 2008. Over the years, several lives have been touched, souls saved, through our evangelistic efforts in crusades and outreaches and the church has been a platform for growing believers to maturity as we remain committed to our ministerial mandate of Declaring God's Praise, Destroying Idolatry and Preaching Holiness.

Our vision is to raise MEN and WOMEN to Perfection.
